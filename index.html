About Us

Built to serve seafood buyers in the Gulf market.

Gulf Salmon & Seafood Exports Ltd is a UK-based seafood export company focused on supplying premium Scottish and British seafood to overseas buyers, with a particular focus on the United Arab Emirates and wider GCC region.

Our aim is to build long-term relationships with seafood distributors, wholesalers, hotels, restaurants and food service companies that require consistent quality and dependable supply.
